Adapt your mutations to fit your game.

  1. Take on a new mutation
  2. Choose a mutation complexity
  3. Choose which mutation you prefer
  4. Add it to your game (Try to get creative to make it fit your game)
  5. Complete the mutation with a short devlog describing what you did
  6. Repeat
  7. When your game is ready, ship it to win prizes
  8. Once you ship, you can add more mutations or start a new game
